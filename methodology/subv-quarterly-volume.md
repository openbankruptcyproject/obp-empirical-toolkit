# Subchapter V Quarterly Volume &mdash; Methodology

## Source

Administrative Office of the U.S. Courts, Bankruptcy Filings Statistics,
Table F-2A. Available quarterly via the AOUSC public website.

## Approach

For each quarter from 2020-Q1 onward:

1. Pull Table F-2A for the quarter ending date
2. Filter to Subchapter V cases (separately reported from standard Ch.11
   in Table F-2A starting Q2 2020)
3. Aggregate to total Subchapter V filings nationally for the quarter

## Notes

- Sub V was created by SBRA effective 2020-02-19, mid-Q1 2020. The Q1 2020
  number (51) reflects ~6 weeks of filings, not a full quarter.
- Q2 2020 is the first full quarter of Sub V availability and shows the
  immediate uptake (311) plus the early CARES Act ceiling expansion effect.
- The CARES Act "sunset" was June 21, 2024 &mdash; mid-Q2 2024. The Q2 2024
  number (478) reflects the half-quarter at $7.5M ceiling and half at the
  reverted ~$3.024M ceiling.

## Limitations

- AOUSC reporting can differ from court-level docket counts for a small number
  of cases (clerical timing, data entry).
- Sub V eligibility determinations made post-confirmation can reclassify
  cases; AOUSC tracks the petition-time classification.
